Song Shaker Standard Library

The Song Shaker App comes with a standard library of 200 songs!

Your personalized library settings are shown below, with active standard songs and inactive standard songs listed separately. You may update your settings using the checkboxes to the right of each song.

You also have the option to add custom songs via the Add Song page and can view your custom songs in the Custom Library.
