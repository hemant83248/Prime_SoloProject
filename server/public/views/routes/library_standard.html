Standard Song Shaker Library

The Song Shaker App comes with a standard library of 200 songs!

You also have the option to add custom songs via the Add Song page and can view your custom songs in the Custom Library.

Your personalized library settings are shown below, with active standard songs and inactive standard songs listed separately.

You may update your settings using the checkboxes to the left of each song.


Active Songs

If you do not want an active song to be available for use in your set lists, please click its corresponding "deactivate" checkbox to the right of the song details.
